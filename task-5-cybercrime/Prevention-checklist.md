The Digital Safety Checklist
•	UPI PIN Privacy: Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money. Scammers often send "Collect Requests"—accepting them deducts money from your account rather than adding to it.
•	Verify Before You Pay: Before hitting 'Proceed' on UPI, double-check the verified merchant name on the confirmation screen to avoid "QR Code Swapping" fraud.
•	Secure Your Socials: Set your Instagram and Facebook profiles to "Private." Limit who can see your personal details to prevent identity theft and doxxing.
•	Enable MFA: Use Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) on all university and personal emails. A password alone is no longer enough to stop modern hackers.
•	Public Wi-Fi Warning: Avoid logging into bank accounts or student portals on campus or cafe Wi-Fi. Use a trusted VPN or your mobile data for sensitive tasks.
•	Think Before You Click: Be wary of "Free Internship" or "Job Offer" links sent via WhatsApp or Telegram; these are often phishing attempts to steal credentials.
•	Update Regularly: Keep your OS and apps updated to patch security vulnerabilities that malware can exploit.
•	Check App Permissions: Regularly audit which apps have access to your camera, microphone, and contacts in your phone settings.
